About Our School

A legacy of excellence in education, character building, and holistic development.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing, and inclusive environment where every student is empowered to learn, grow, and thrive. We aim to foster a love for learning, critical thinking, and social responsibility, preparing our students to become global citizens and future leaders.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence, recognized for our commitment to holistic development, innovation, and character building. We envision a community where students, teachers, and parents collaborate to achieve the highest standards of academic and personal growth.

Our History

Founded in 1995, School Demo started with a humble vision and a handful of students. Over the decades, we have grown into a premier institution producing leaders in various fields.

1995

Established with 50 students and 5 teachers.

2005

Expanded to a new campus with state-of-the-art facilities.

2015

Introduced international curriculum and advanced labs.

Present

Educating over 1200 students with a focus on innovation.
